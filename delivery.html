Important Notes

Production Time

Delivery timelines start from artwork approval, not order placement. Ensure your artwork is ready and approved promptly to avoid delays.

Delivery Coverage

We deliver island-wide including Sentosa and Jurong Island. For restricted areas (military bases), additional arrangements may be required.

Public Holidays

We do not deliver on Singapore public holidays. Orders placed on or before a public holiday will be dispatched on the next working day.

Packaging

All products are carefully packaged to prevent damage during transit. Large format items are rolled in protective tubes or boxed flat.
