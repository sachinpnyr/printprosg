Artwork & Files

We accept PDF (preferred), AI, EPS, and high-resolution JPG/PNG. PDF with embedded fonts and CMYK colour mode is strongly recommended for best results. Please follow our Artwork Guidelines for full specifications.

All files must be in CMYK colour mode. RGB files will be automatically converted to CMYK, which may cause colour shifts. For accurate colour reproduction, always design in CMYK from the start.

A minimum of 300 DPI at actual print size is required. For large format products (banners, foam board), 150 DPI at actual size is acceptable. Low-resolution files will result in blurry prints.

A bleed is an extra 3mm of artwork that extends beyond the trim edge. It ensures that after cutting, there are no white borders on the edges. All background colours and images that touch the edge must extend into the bleed area. See our Artwork Guidelines for templates.

The safe zone is 3mm inside the trim edge. All important content (text, logos, key graphics) must be kept within the safe zone to avoid being cut off during trimming.
