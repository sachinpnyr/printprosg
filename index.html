Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our printing services.

What is your turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 3–5 working days. We also offer express same-day or next-day printing for urgent orders at an additional charge. Contact us to discuss your timeline.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, AI, PSD, EPS, and high-resolution JPG/PNG files. For best results, submit print-ready PDFs with 3mm bleed and CMYK colour mode. Our team can also help you prepare files.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! We have an in-house design team that can create or refine your artwork. Design fees vary by complexity. Orders above $150 include a free basic design service.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum quantities vary by product. Name cards start from 50 pieces, flyers from 100 pieces, and t-shirts from 10 pieces. Contact us for specific product minimums.

Do you deliver to my location?

Yes, we offer delivery across Singapore. We also ship internationally to Malaysia, UAE, India, and other countries. Delivery charges apply based on order size and destination.

How do I get a quote?

Simply fill in the contact form below, WhatsApp us at +65 9875 1606, or call us directly. Tell us the product, quantity, size, and any special requirements and we'll get back to you within 2 hours.
