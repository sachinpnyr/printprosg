# PrintProSG Visual Audit — Detailed Notes

## DESKTOP FINDINGS (1440px)

### 1. Navbar
- Logo: Print Pro with coloured ink strokes — recognisable and clean
- Search bar: centred, well-proportioned
- Phone + icons + CTA: right-aligned, balanced
- Category tabs: "Most Popular" active with red underline — functional
- Trust bar: Best Price / Design Services / Delivery / Credit Terms / Rating
- ISSUE: Cart badge shows "0" — should be hidden when empty
- ISSUE: Category tabs still show old data-filter attributes not applied (need to verify live)
- RATING: 8/10

### 2. Hero
- Headline "Premium Printing, Delivered Fast." — bold, impactful
- "Delivered Fast." in red — strong visual accent
- Sub-text: clear and concise
- Two CTAs: filled red + outlined
- Trust row: Same-day / Free design / 500+ clients
- Right side: 4 product cards in 2x2 grid with prices
- Background: faint hand/paper texture — subtle and premium
- ISSUE: Hero is left-aligned text with right product grid — feels slightly unbalanced on wide desktop
- ISSUE: Background image is very faint — barely adds value
- RATING: 8/10

### 3. Reviews
- 4 review cards in a row (Aimi, Vikram, Amirah, Kim)
- Stars, avatar, name, time, truncated review text, Google badge
- Rating header: 4.8/5 from 200+ reviews with stars
- Carousel dots and arrows visible
- ISSUE: Review text is hard-truncated mid-sentence — no ellipsis visible, looks broken
- ISSUE: Section very tall (769px) — too much top/bottom padding
- ISSUE: Cookie banner overlaps bottom of section on first load
- RATING: 7/10

### 4. About
- Left: heading + 2 paragraphs + stats (34+, 500+, 50+)
- Right: 4 feature cards in 2x2 grid (Turnaround, Quality, Customisation, Experts)
- Stats: large bold numbers — impactful
- "Get a Free Quote" CTA button at bottom
- ISSUE: Left column text is very long — two dense paragraphs feel heavy
- ISSUE: Feature cards have red icons that look like warning symbols (bolt, shield, layers, person)
- ISSUE: "Get a Free Quote" button is left-aligned under the text — inconsistent with section layout
- RATING: 7/10

### 5. How It Works
- 4 steps in a row: Upload → Approve → Print → Deliver
- Red circular icons, step numbers (01-04), titles, descriptions
- Clean horizontal layout with connector line
- ISSUE: Step descriptions are very generic ("Upload Your Design", "Approve Your Proof")
- ISSUE: The connector line between steps is not visible in screenshot
- ISSUE: Cards have too much vertical padding — section is 570px for 4 simple steps
- RATING: 7.5/10

### 6. Services (CRITICAL ISSUE)
- 15 cards in 3-column grid — filter tabs working
- Section is 2554px tall — extremely tall
- Cards have image + title + description + price + CTA
- ISSUE: Section is way too tall — 2554px for 15 cards is excessive
- ISSUE: The expanded 15-card grid was added but the old 6-card grid may still exist (need to verify)
- ISSUE: No "Load More" or pagination — all 15 cards shown at once even with filter
- ISSUE: Services section header says "Our Printing Services" — redundant with category tabs
- RATING: 5/10 (functional but too tall)

### 7. Portfolio
- Clean 3-column grid with portfolio images
- Filter tabs: All / Cards & Stationery / Print Materials / Display & Signage / Apparel & Gifts
- Images are high quality (name cards, booklets, flyers, banners, wedding stationery)
- Cookie banner overlapping bottom
- ISSUE: Cookie banner blocks bottom row of portfolio on first load
- ISSUE: Portfolio filter tabs duplicate the nav category tabs concept — redundant
- RATING: 8.5/10 (best-looking section)

### 8. Locations
- 3 cards: Singapore (HQ badge), UAE, India
- Each card: flag, country, company name, address, phone, hours, WhatsApp + Directions buttons
- Google Map embed at bottom
- Clean card layout with rounded corners
- ISSUE: "Fanan Advertising LLC" and "Priya Printers" as partner names — visitors may be confused about who they're dealing with
- ISSUE: Map embed visible but cut off — takes up a lot of space
- RATING: 8/10

### 9. FAQ
- Clean accordion layout on grey background
- 5 questions visible: turnaround time, file formats, design services, minimum order, delivery
- Large "+" icon on right of each row
- ISSUE: FAQ questions have too much spacing between them — each row is ~80px tall when closed
- ISSUE: Only 5 questions visible — section feels sparse for an FAQ
- ISSUE: Background is light grey — breaks visual flow from white sections
- RATING: 7.5/10

### 10. Contact
- Left: heading "Let's Print Something Great Together" + description + contact details (call, WhatsApp, address, hours, email)
- Right: form with Full Name, Email, Phone/WhatsApp, Service Needed, Message, Send Enquiry button
- "WE ACCEPT: PayNow + Bank Transfer" visible at bottom
- ISSUE: Left column contact info is good but the section title is very long — wraps to 3 lines
- ISSUE: Form has no visual card/shadow — blends into background
- ISSUE: "Send Enquiry" button is full-width red — good prominence
- RATING: 8/10
